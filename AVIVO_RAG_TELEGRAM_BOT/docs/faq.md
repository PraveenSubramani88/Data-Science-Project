# IT Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 1. How do I reset my password?

Employees can reset their password through the company self-service portal. If the portal is unavailable, contact the IT Help Desk. Passwords must contain at least 8 characters, including uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

## 2. How often should I change my password?

Employees are required to change their passwords every 90 days. Password reuse is not allowed for the last 5 passwords.

## 3. What should I do if my account is locked?

If your account becomes locked after multiple failed login attempts, wait 15 minutes and try again. If the issue persists, contact the IT Help Desk for assistance.

## 4. How can I request a new laptop?

Employees can submit a hardware request through the IT Service Portal. Manager approval is required before the request is processed.

## 5. What is the standard laptop provided to employees?

New employees are typically issued a company-approved laptop with at least 16 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD storage, and the latest supported operating system.

## 6. How do I connect to the company VPN?

Install the approved VPN client, enter your company credentials, and complete multi-factor authentication (MFA). VPN access is required when accessing company resources outside the office network.

## 7. What should I do if I suspect a phishing email?

Do not click any links or download attachments. Report the email immediately using the "Report Phishing" option in the email client or forward it to the IT Security team.

## 8. Can I install software on my company laptop?

Employees may only install software that has been approved by the IT department. Unauthorized software installations are prohibited.

## 9. What is the internet usage policy?

Company internet access is provided for business purposes. Limited personal use is allowed as long as it does not affect productivity, security, or network performance.

## 10. How do I access shared company files?

Shared files can be accessed through the company file management system using your employee credentials. Access permissions are granted based on job responsibilities.

## 11. What should I do if my laptop is lost or stolen?

Report the incident to the IT Help Desk and your manager immediately. The IT team will remotely secure or wipe the device if necessary.

## 12. How can I contact the IT Help Desk?

The IT Help Desk can be contacted through:

* Email: [ithelpdesk@company.com](mailto:ithelpdesk@company.com)
* Phone: +1-800-555-1234
* Service Portal: Internal IT Service Portal

## 13. What are the IT Help Desk working hours?

The IT Help Desk operates from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Critical incidents can be reported 24/7 through the emergency support line.

## 14. What is Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)?

MFA is a security process that requires users to verify their identity using a second authentication method, such as a mobile app or security code, in addition to their password.

## 15. How long does it take to resolve IT tickets?

Ticket resolution times vary by priority:

* Critical: Within 4 hours
* High: Within 1 business day
* Medium: Within 3 business days
* Low: Within 5 business days
